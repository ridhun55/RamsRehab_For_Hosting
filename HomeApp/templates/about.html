About us

RAMS Rehab is a Pain clinic established in 2020. This Rehab Center is completely focusing on the body pain and it's management. As we have noticed that medical professionals doesn't give much importance to pain management, rather than using frequent pain killers. So we want to solve the most relevant problem facing by our society ' Pain '.
By integrating traditional and most advanced techniques along with proper diet and exercise plans, which actually works for a countless people who are suffering from 'Pain'. And we would like to continue our passion and journey along with you " Who needs help in Pain".
