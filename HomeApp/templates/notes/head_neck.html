Head And Neck

Cervical radiculopathy Cervicogenic headache/ migraine Stiff neck Upper cross syndrome
Therapy 1

Cervical radiculopathy

< class="lead pl-4"> - Neck pain radiating to one or both upper limb
- Tingling and numbness
- Reduced range of motion of neck
- Weakness in hand

Special Test

- Spurling test
- Upper limb dermatome
- Upper limb myotome
- MRI

Treatment

- Electrotherapy
- IFT
- MFR
- Mechanical traction

Therapeutic Exercise

- Chin tuck
- Median nerve glide
- Deep neck flexor strengthening
- Thoracic rotation on a form roll
- Quadripod thoracic rotation

Therapy 2

Cervicogenic headache / Migraine

- Neck pain associated with headache
- Execerbated during head movement
- During cold, sunlight, stress

Special Test

- Palpation of cervical trigger points

Treatment

- Electrotherapy
- Dry needling
- Needle stimulation
- IFT

Therapeutic Exercise

- Wall angels
- Ball on wall rotation
- Deep neck flexor strengthening
- DRY needling

Therapy 3

Stiffneck

- Turning body along with the neck
- Reduced range of motion

Treatment

- Electrotherapy
- Dry needle
- MFR

Therapeutic Exercise

- Manual release
- Cervical rotation using a towel
- Neck stretching
- Scalene stretching
- Trapezius stretch
- Levator scapula stretching
- Pectoralis stretching

Therapy 4

Upper cross syndrome

- Forward neck posture
- Reduced cervical lordosis
- Radiating acheing pain in upper limb

Special Test

- Deep neck flexor MMT

Treatment

- Electrotherapy
- MFR
- Needle stimulation

Therapeutic Exercise

- Stretching
- A, upper trapezius
- B, pectoralis
- Strengthening
- A, Deep neck flexor
- B, lower trapezius
