Therapy 1

WRIST SPRAIN

- Fall on outstretched hand get a sprain between couple of your wrist bones

Treatment

- PRICE protocol
- P- Protection
- R- Rest
- I- Ice
- C- Compression
- E- Elevation

Therapeutic Exercise

- Wrist strenghthening
- Wrist isometric
- Wrist flexor strenghthening
- Wrist extensor strenghthening

Therapy 2

CARPEL TUNNEL SYNDROME

- Median nerve compression in carpal tunnel
- Difficulty in wringing cloths
- Inability to hold a cup of water
- Couldn't grip things or pain during gripping
- Numbness and tingling feeling in the hand during typing or wrist movements
- Night pain

Special Test

- Tinnel's test
- Wrist flexion median nerve compression test
- Phalens test
- Modified phalens test
- Closed fist lumbrical provocation test

Treatment

- Electrotherapy
- Ultra sound
- LASER
- Dry Needling

Therapeutic Exercise

- Meadian nerve glide
- ADL modification
- Myofascil release
- Manual therapy
- Stretching

Therapy 3

WRIST STIFFNESS

- Common after colle's fracture

Treatment

- Electrotherapy
- Heat therapy

Therapeutic Exercise

- Wrist isometrics
- Wrist mobilization
- Mallet twist
- Table top wrist stretch

Therapy 4

THUMB ARTHRITIS

- Pain, Swelling, decresed strength and ROM
- Difficulty in doing simple task such as turning door knobs and opening jars

Special Test

- Grind test

Treatment

- Electrotherapy
- Wax therapy
- Dry Needling

Therapeutic Exercise

- ADL Modification
- Joint mobilization
- Thumb passive ROM
- Self thumb traction
- Rubber band exercise
- Squeeze ball press
- Taping
- Thumb spica splint

Therapy 5

DE QUERVAIN'S TENOYNOVITS

- Thickening of thumb's tendon
- Restricted back and forth movement
- Pain and tenderness
- Decresed movements of thumb

Special Test

- Finkelstein test

Treatment

- Electrotherapy
- Dry Needling
- Ultra sound

Therapeutic Exercise

- Rubber band exercise
- Squeeze ball pressing
- Thumb spica splint
